WORKSHOP

We're launching a series of practical workshops on development in January 2020. We'll teach you how to know your way around software, share the latest news, and help to start.

First workshop will familiarize you with cross-platform mobile development toolkit called Flutter. Join if you're aspiring to learn how to develop mobile apps and master new tools, but unsure what to start with.

ABOUT WHAT
Platform perks, intro to Dart language and creating your own apps. Bonus: intro to (fantastic) animation.
FOR WHOM
Students and IT professionals that try to decide if they want to work with mobile app development fast.
WHY
To cover cross-platform development bases quickly. Understand pros and cons if it using workshop format.

WHEN AND WHERE

Where

About the course
Duration: 2 weeks. Comprises 4 2-hour modules twice a week.
